Biography

I am Zeyi Zhang, a senior student at the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science(EECS), Peking University. After graduation, I will go to the School of Intelligence Science and Technology to pursue a Ph.D degree advised by Prof. Libin Liu.

My research interests mainly include Co-Speech Gesture Generation and Generative Model. Thanks to my guide and collaborator, Tenglong Ao, we are working on generating natural and realistic human behaviour.
